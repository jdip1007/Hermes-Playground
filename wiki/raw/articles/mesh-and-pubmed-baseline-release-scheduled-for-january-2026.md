        <h1>MeSH and PubMed Baseline Release Scheduled for January 2026</h1>
 

<a href="https://techbull.nlm.nih.gov/vivisimo/cgi-bin/query-meta?v%3Aproject=technical-bulletin-date&sortby=Date-decending&query=&binning-state=product==MeSH"><button class="usa-tag padding-y-05 padding-x-1">Mesh</button></a> <a href="https://techbull.nlm.nih.gov/vivisimo/cgi-bin/query-meta?v%3Aproject=technical-bulletin-date&sortby=Date-decending&query=&binning-state=product==PubMed"><button class="usa-tag padding-y-05 padding-x-1">Pubmed</button></a>    
        <p class="text-base">MeSH and PubMed Baseline Release Scheduled for January 2026. NLM Tech Bull. 2025 Nov-Dec;(<a href="/pubs/techbull/nd25/nd25_issue_cover.html">467</a>):b4.</p>
 
 <p class="border-bottom-1px border-primary grid-col-6 padding-bottom-3 margin-bottom-0">December 09, 2025 [posted] 
 
 
        
 
  
 </p>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="grid-row grid-gap">
    

<div class="desktop:grid-col-6 tablet:grid-offset-1 padding-y-3 tablet:grid-col-12 mobile:grid-col-12">   
    
<p>You will be able to&nbsp;<a href="https://www.nlm.nih.gov/databases/download/mesh.html">download</a>&nbsp;2026 MeSH from links on the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.nlm.nih.gov/databases/download/data_distrib_main.html">NLM Data Distribution page</a> for MeSH Data the week of January 5, 2026. An announcement with further details for the release of 2026 MeSH XML Data will be announced in December 2025.</p>
<p>The updated MeSH 2026 DTDs are available for download at <a href="https://www.nlm.nih.gov/databases/download/mesh.html">Download MeSH Data</a>.<br /><br /></p>
The update of MEDLINE citations in PubMed with 2026 MeSH is delayed until early 2026. A timeline for this update and the release of the 2026 baseline will be announced in a future Technical Bulletin article.
