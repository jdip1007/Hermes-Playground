        <h1>PubMed Update: Related Citation Links in Search Results</h1>
 

<a href="https://techbull.nlm.nih.gov/vivisimo/cgi-bin/query-meta?v%3Aproject=technical-bulletin-date&sortby=Date-decending&query=&binning-state=product==PubMed"><button class="usa-tag padding-y-05 padding-x-1">Pubmed</button></a> 

        <p class="text-base">PubMed Update: Related Citation Links in Search Results. NLM Tech Bull. 2024 May-Jun;(<a href="/pubs/techbull/mj24/mj24_issue_cover.html">458</a>):e6.</p>


         
        <p class="border-bottom-1px border-primary grid-col-6 padding-bottom-3 margin-bottom-0">June 13, 2024 [posted] 
       


</p>
          
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="grid-row grid-gap">
 
   
  
<div class="tablet:grid-col-10 tablet:grid-offset-1 mobile:grid-col-12">
 
   

       <p>The four PubMed <a href="https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/help/#comment-correction">comment correction types</a> labeled in search results now also include a link to the related citation in the summary display: 
<ul>
<li>Update in.</li>
<li>Retraction in.</li>
<li>Retracted and republished in.</li>
<li>Corrected and republished in.</li></ul></p>
<p>For example, a <a href="https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/about/nihpreprints/">preprint</a> citation that is linked to a published journal article now includes a link from the search results to the published article's PubMed citation (Figure 1).</p> 

<a name="fig1"></a>
<div class="figure"><br /> 
<img src="/pubs/techbull/mj24/graphics/pubmed_citation_fig1.png" alt="screenshot of a search result with the related 'Update In' citation linked in the fourth line." /><br /> 
<strong>Figure 1. Example preprint citation with linked "Update In" citation.</strong></div>

<p>These new links appear in the search results summary display for larger screens such as desktops or tablets. On smaller screens and mobile devices, this information continues to appear as a brief label.</p>
<p>All available related citations continue to be linked on a citation's abstract page. The complete list of comment correction types found in PubMed is available from <a href="https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/help/#comment-correction">PubMed User Guide: Comment Correction Type</a>.</p>
