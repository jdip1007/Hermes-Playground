## Overview

Method to extract HMW genomic DNA from human brain tissue using QIAGEN MagAttract HMW DNA Kit, automatable on KingFisher Flex or Duo Prime systems.[1] Yields ~8–15 µg DNA from 25 mg frozen brain tissue.[1] Libraries prepared with Ligation Sequencing Kit, optionally sheared with FastPrep-96 before PromethION sequencing.[1]

## Key Steps

- Start with 25 mg frozen brain tissue[1]
- Use QIAGEN MagAttract HMW DNA Kit for extraction (automatable)[1]
- Optional: shear with FastPrep-96 for higher throughput at expense of read length[1]
- Flow cell washing recommended to maximize output[1]
