## Overview

Method for DNA extraction from soil samples using QIAGEN DNeasy PowerMax Soil Kit (formerly MO BIO PowerMax).[1] Start with 8 g of material — do not exceed kit capacity of 10 g to avoid membrane blockage.[1] Tested modifications (bead-beating at 65°C or overnight at room temperature) showed no improvement in yield vs standard protocol.[1]

## Key Steps

- Start with ~8 g soil sample[1]
- Perform lysis per manufacturer's manual, page 8[1]
- If no vortex adapter for Falcon tubes: tape tubes to vortex mixer for bead-beating step (ensure not full)[1]
- Follow QIAGEN DNeasy PowerMax Soil Kit protocol[1]
- Elute in TE buffer[1]
