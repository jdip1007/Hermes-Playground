### whole-blood-nad-stability

**Source:** Trętowicz et al., "Whole-blood NAD+ levels do not vary with age or lifestyle interventions," *Nature Metabolism*, 2026.

Multi-cohort analysis challenging the NAD+ decline hypothesis in aging research:
- Whole-blood NAD+ levels remain stable across adult lifespan (no age-related decline)
- No significant changes after diverse lifestyle interventions (diet, exercise, fasting)
- 7 independent cohorts, 300+ participants total
- Mass spectrometry-based metabolomics (LC-MS/MS) methodology
- Implications: questions rationale for NAD+ precursor supplements as anti-aging interventions; suggests blood NAD+ is poor biomarker for cellular aging

### nad-precursors-supplementation

**Context:** Overview of NAD+ precursor supplement market and evidence base.

Comprehensive coverage of NR/NMN supplementation landscape:
- NAD+ salvage pathway mechanisms (NR → NMN → NAD+)
- Evidence from animal studies vs human trials (mixed results)
- Market size, regulatory status (FDA warnings on NMN), quality control issues
- Implications from Trętowicz et al. findings — blood NAD+ may not be meaningful biomarker; tissue-specific effects remain possible
