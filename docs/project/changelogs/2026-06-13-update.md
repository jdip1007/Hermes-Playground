## New Pages Created

### Cognitive Disengagement Syndrome (CDS/SCT)

**Concept page:** `concepts/cognitive-disengagement-syndrome.md` (~19KB)
- Comprehensive overview of CDS/SCT as distinct syndrome from ADHD
- History from 1798 (Crichton) through modern research
- Diagnostic criteria and symptom dimensions (daydreamy-spacey, sluggish-lethargic)
- Neurobiology: fMRI evidence showing superior parietal lobule involvement vs prefrontal cortex in ADHD
- Treatment evidence: Atomoxetine effective, methylphenidate poor response across RCTs
- Comorbidity patterns: Internalizing disorders (anxiety, depression) vs externalizing in ADHD
- Controversy section documenting skepticism from Frances and Lee
- 116 inline citations to peer-reviewed sources

**Comparison page:** `comparisons/ces-vs-adhd-comparison.md` (~11KB)
- Differential diagnosis framework with symptom matrices
- Core mechanism distinction: externalized hyperactivity (ADHD) vs internalized hypoactivity (CDS)
- Treatment response differences detailed with medication mechanisms
- Comorbidity pattern comparison
- Diagnostic challenges and red flags for CDS misdiagnosis as ADHD
- 89 inline citations
