## Overview

Method to extract genomic DNA from human blood collected on Whatman FTA cards using QIAGEN EZ2 Connect automation device with tissue protocol.[1] Blood applied to FTA cards upon arrival in lab, cells counted before application for efficiency assessment.[1] Optimal results: 10 discs of 3 mm diameter each, pre-lysed for one hour, processed in 15 minutes on EZ2 Connect.[1]

## Key Steps

- Punch 3 mm discs from FTA card (maximum 10 for consistent recovery)[1]
- Pre-lyse discs for 1 hour[1]
- Load into QIAGEN EZ2 Connect using tissue protocol (15 min purification)[1]
- Prepare library with Ligation Sequencing Kit, sequence on PromethION[1]
