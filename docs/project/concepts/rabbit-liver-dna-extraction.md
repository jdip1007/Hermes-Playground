## Overview

Method to extract HMW genomic DNA from rabbit liver tissue using QIAGEN Blood and Cell Culture DNA Midi Kit.[1] Up to 80 mg liver tissue ground with tweezers/scalpel, lysed in G2 buffer with RNase A and Proteinase K overnight at 50°C.[1]

## Key Steps

- Grind up to 80 mg rabbit liver tissue into pulp[1]
- Add to 15 ml Falcon tube containing 9.5 ml G2 buffer + 19 µl RNase A (100 mg/ml)[1]
- Add 250 µl Proteinase K, vortex thoroughly for efficient lysis[1]
- Incubate overnight at 50°C[1]
- If lysate not homogeneous after incubation: centrifuge 2000 × g, 10 min, 4°C; discard pellet, retain supernatant[1]
