## Overview

Method to extract and sequence HMW genomic DNA from Atlantic salmon (*Salmo salar*) blood.[1] Blood stored in 90% ethanol at −80°C before extraction yields **highest sequencing output** compared to tissue samples.[1] Uses MagAttract HMW DNA kit. Optional shearing with Covaris g-TUBE or Megaruptor increases output but reduces read N50.[1]

## Key Steps

- Start with salmon blood in ~90% ethanol, stored at −80°C[1]
- Invert 10 times to mix before extraction[1]
- Extract using MagAttract HMW DNA kit[1]
- Optional: shear for increased output (reduced N50 trade-off)[1]
