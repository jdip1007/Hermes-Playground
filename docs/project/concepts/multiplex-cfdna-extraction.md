## Overview

Method to extract cell-free DNA from 12 human blood samples collected in EDTA K₂ vacuum tubes using QIAGEN QIAamp MinElute ccfDNA Midi Kit.[1] Blood processed while fresh to avoid gDNA contamination from stored samples.[1] Tested cfDNA inputs of 6, 4, and 2 ng per barcode — **≥6 ng provides optimal flow cell performance**.[1]

## Key Steps

- Collect blood in EDTA K₂ tubes or use human plasma[1]
- Process while fresh to minimize gDNA contamination risk[1]
- Extract cfDNA from 1–5 ml serum/plasma using QIAamp MinElute ccfDNA Midi Kit[1]
- Prepare for multiplex sequencing with Ligation Sequencing Kit V14 (SQK-NBD114.24) on PromethION[1]
