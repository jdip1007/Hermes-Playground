## Overview

Community-contributed method for extracting DNA from *M. tuberculosis* solid culture in Löwenstein–Jensen medium.[1] Uses ThermoFisher PrepMan® Ultra Sample Preparation Reagent with glass bead beating, followed by AMPure XP bead clean-up.[1] Based on Bainomugisa et al. (2018) — complete nanopore-only assembly of XDR *M. tuberculosis* Beijing lineage strain identifying novel genetic variation in PE/PPE gene regions.[1]

## Key Steps

- Dispense 700 µl PrepMan Ultra reagent into tube with glass beads (beads ≤50% liquid level)[1]
- Add one loop-full of culture, twirl loop to remove[1]
- Heat at 95°C for 15 minutes[1]
- Shear in BioSpec BeadBeater: 3 pulses × 40 seconds at 6.0 m/s[1]
- Centrifuge 10 min at 13,000 rpm, transfer 450 µl supernatant[1]
