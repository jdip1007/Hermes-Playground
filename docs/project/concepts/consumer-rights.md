# Consumer Rights

Legal protections and rights available to consumers when purchasing goods and services.

## Overview

Consumer rights protect individuals from unfair business practices, defective products, and fraudulent services.

## Key Areas

- **Product Liability**: Protection against defective products
- **Service Quality**: Rights regarding service standards
- **Refund Policies**: Legal requirements for refunds and returns
- **Consumer Protection Laws**: Regulations preventing unfair practices
