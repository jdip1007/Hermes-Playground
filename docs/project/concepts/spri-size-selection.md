## Overview

Method for size-selecting already purified DNA to enrich for fragments above 1.5–2 kb.[1] Based on work by Schalamun and Schwessinger with modifications.[1] Uses custom SPRI beads (0.7× volume of Agencourt AMPure XP beads) — optimal selection for >2 kb fragments, improving median read length of fragmented gDNA.[1] Works with both genomic DNA and PCR samples.[1]

## Custom Buffer Preparation

- **Critical**: accurately pipette 548 µl of 40% w/v PEG 8000 (use wide-bore 1 ml tips)[1]
- Mix: Tris-HCl (1 M → 10 mM final), EDTA (0.5 M → 2.5 mM final), NaCl (5 M → 100 mM final), PEG 8000 (40% w/v → 16% w/v final)[1]
