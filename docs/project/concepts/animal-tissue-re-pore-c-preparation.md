## Overview

Protocol describing cryo-grinding and DNA extraction from *Rattus norvegicus* (Sprague Dawley) brain and muscle tissues for restriction enzyme Pore-C (RE-Pore-C) chromatin conformation analysis.[1]

## Key Steps

- **Cryo-grinding** (~20 min hands-on): Pre-chill mortar/pestle at −80°C ≥30 minutes[1]
- Dissect tissue into <1 cm² pieces, freeze with liquid nitrogen in chilled mortar[1]
- Grind frozen tissue to fine powder while minimizing thawing[1]
- Proceed to RE-Pore-C extraction protocol[1]
