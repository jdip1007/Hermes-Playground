## Overview

Method to extract and sequence HMW genomic DNA from Atlantic salmon (*Salmo salar*) brain, heart, liver, spleen, or fin tissue.[1] Tissue samples generate **lower sequencing output** than blood stored in 90% ethanol.[1] Uses MagAttract HMW DNA kit. Optional shearing with Covaris g-TUBE or Megaruptor increases output but reduces read N50.[1]

## Key Steps

- Start with ~25 mg tissue (brain, heart, liver, spleen, or fin) stored at −80°C[1]
- Cut into small pieces and grind using tweezers/scalpel[1]
- Extract using MagAttract HMW DNA kit[1]
- Optional: shear for increased output (reduced N50 trade-off)[1]
