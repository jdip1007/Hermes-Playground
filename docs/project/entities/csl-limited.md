# CSL Limited

## Overview

CSL Limited is an Australian multinational biotechnology company headquartered in Melbourne [1]. It is one of the largest plasma-derived biopharmaceutical companies globally [1].

## Key Facts [1]

- **Founded**: 1916 as Commonwealth Serum Laboratories (government-owned)
- **Privatized**: 1994, listed on ASX and NYSE
- **CEO**: Paul Hudson (since 2019)
- **Employees**: ~27,000 worldwide
- **Revenue**: ~AUD 15B+ annually

## Business Segments [1]

- **CSL Behring** — Plasma-derived therapies (IVIG, albumin, coagulation factors)
- **Seqirus** — Vaccine manufacturing (influenza, pandemic preparedness)
- **CSL Vifor** — Renal care (formerly, now part of Vifor after 2022 spin-off)

## Competitive Position [1]

- Global leader in plasma fractionation and IVIG manufacturing
- One of the largest plasma collection networks worldwide
- Beneficiary of chronic plasma supply shortages driving pricing power
