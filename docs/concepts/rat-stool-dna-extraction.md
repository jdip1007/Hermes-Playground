## Overview

Method to extract genomic DNA from rat stool pellets using QIAGEN DNeasy Blood & Tissue Kit with custom lysis buffer per Maudet et al.[1] Lysis buffer composition: 100 mM Tris-HCl, 100 mM EDTA, 10 mM NaCl, 1% N-lauroyl-sarcosine, pH 7.5.[1]

## Key Steps

- Transfer 3 rat stool pellets to 20 ml scintillation tube + 500 µl lysis buffer[1]
- Rotate on HulaMixer at 20 rpm for 15 min at room temperature (or invert every minute)[1]
- Transfer ~200–300 µl supernatant to 2 ml tube, avoiding solid matter[1]
- Add 200 µl buffer AL + 20 µl Proteinase K + 4 µl RNase A from DNeasy kit[1]
