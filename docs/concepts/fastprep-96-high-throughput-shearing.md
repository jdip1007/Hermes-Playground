## Overview

Protocol for shearing up to 192 samples of DNA using the FastPrep-96 High Throughput Bead Beating Grinder and Lysis System or 2010 Geno/Grinder.[1] Generates read length N50 distributions of 10, 12.5, 15 and 20 kb.[1] Uses no beads for DNA processing — purely mechanical shearing.[1]

## Recommended Settings (15 kb target)

- **Speed**: 1600 SPM[1]
- **Time**: 5 minutes[1]
- **Concentration**: 30 ng/µl in 120 µl volume[1]
- Further optimization may be needed depending on sample quality and desired fragment size[1]
