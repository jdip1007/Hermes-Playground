## Overview

Method to extract genomic DNA from environmental water samples (pond water example).[1] Multiple extraction kits tested against ZymoBIOMICS Microbial Community Standard — **ZymoBIOMICS DNA Miniprep Kit** yielded community representation closest to expected control.[1] Other methods (Quick-DNA HMW MagBead, QIAGEN MagAttract, Puregene, PowerWater) showed deviation from expected species distribution.[1]

## Validation

- Filtered water through vacuum, extracted gDNA directly from filter[1]
- Validated with residential garden pond sample containing known fish species[1]
- Tested both PCR-amplified (Rapid PCR Barcoding Kit) and non-amplified (Ligation Sequencing Kit) approaches[1]
